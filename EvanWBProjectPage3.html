Some fiction!

And for my final act, I will be composing a few pieces of fiction! Last year, while completing the final 3 credits of Master's Degree in English Literature at Seton Hall, I was compelled by circumstance beyond my control to enroll in a fiction workshop. I've never written fiction before, but I quite enjoyed dabbling in the art of storytelling with the support of my instructor and and a classroom full of creative writers. I hope you enjoy the fruits of that labor.
